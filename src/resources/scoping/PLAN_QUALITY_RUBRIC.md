# Plan Quality Rubric

Rate each 0-5:

- Clarity of objectives
- Appropriateness of scope
- Constraint alignment (cost/time/calls)
- Tool selection suitability
- Risk awareness and mitigation
- Evidence coverage plan
- Parallelization opportunities
- Verification and validation steps
- Efficiency (expected time-to-signal)
- Maintainability and adaptability

Guidance:
- Score < 3.0 overall: revise before execution
- Score 3.0-4.0: minor revisions suggested
- Score >= 4.0: approved for execution
